Inside Out Meme: Humor & Commentary

Project Overview

This meme explores the trend of using ChatGPT or AI for completing school assignments. It references a popular Inside Out Characters meme: "Do you ever look at someone and wonder what is going on inside their head?".

Concept & Humor

  • Scenario: Riley’s mother worries about her daughter’s anxiety, while inside Riley’s mind, she considers using ChatGPT to write a 4000-word paper.
  • Characters: Joy, Sadness, Anger, and Disgust hesitate about the idea, while Anxiety gives a “Good Decision” nod.
  • Message: A playful critique of AI-assisted academic work and how students navigate it internally.

Reflections

This project was a fun exploration of meme culture combined with thoughtful commentary on AI usage in education. It demonstrates creativity in visual storytelling while remaining humorous and relatable.
