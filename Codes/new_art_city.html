Virtual Space Project: New Art City

Project Overview

This project explores virtual space design within the platform New Art City, integrating custom 3D assets modeled in Blender to create a relaxing, immersive environment that functions as a digital art installation.

Category:

Virtual Environment Design / 3D Modeling

Tools Used:

Blender, New Art City

Focus:

Lighting, Geometry, and Composition

Design Process & Description

I envisioned the environment as a modern, tranquil suite filled with gentle lighting, organic shapes, and calming color tones. Using Blender, I created 3D models such as couches, plants, and geometric polygons to populate the scene.

Once the models were finalized, I imported them into New Art City, where I adjusted light sources and camera angles to enhance depth and atmosphere. The use of a sky blue background and reflective surfaces gave the space an airy, peaceful aesthetic.

The final space offers an immersive and meditative experience, blending architecture and digital art to create a welcoming virtual environment.
